About My Career

As a developer and general technology enthusiast, I strive to be as well rounded as possible in the tech industry. Because of this, I feel my experience has allowed me to become very familiar with every step within the software development lifecycle. I am confident, at this point in my career, I can fit within any step of the software development lifecycle. In my early days, I started out as a help desk technician and eventually entered into the industry as a Technical Support Engineer. After some years doing that, I decided I wanted to learn to code. At this point I entered the field of QA and QA Automation. As a QA Engineer and rising to the senior level, I decided to take on additional tasks and entered the world of DevOps. Ultimately, I realized what I also really enjoyed besides using technologies to build software. I enjoyed coding and developing software. So here I am... currently a full stack software developer.

Here are a couple of small side projects I worked on recently: World War 2 Trivia Game and Word Guess Game.
